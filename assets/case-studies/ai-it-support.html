AI-led IT Support Platform

Reimagining internal IT support using agentic AI and service-driven design principles.

  • Role: Experience Designer
  • Timeline: 4 weeks
  • Type: Concept / Enterprise

Context

Employees struggled to resolve IT issues efficiently due to fragmented knowledge, unclear ownership, and ticket-heavy workflows.

The problem

Traditional IT portals focus on ticket submission rather than resolution, creating friction and unnecessary handoffs.

My role

I led the experience design, defining user journeys, interaction models, and AI-assisted workflows in collaboration with stakeholders.

Design process

  • User journey mapping
  • Pain point synthesis
  • Concept modelling for agentic AI
  • Low-fidelity interaction design

The solution

An AI-led support experience that proactively guides users, surfaces relevant knowledge, and clarifies next steps.

Impact & learnings

The concept reframed IT support from ticket handling to problem resolution, improving clarity and trust.
