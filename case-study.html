The Problem
Context and pain points.

The organisation's IT support is overwhelmed with repetitive requests; users struggle to find self-serve answers, increasing traffic to live support and reducing productivity. Generative AI was chosen as a lever to surface answers, summarise knowledge and reduce tickets.

Lack of visibility created mistrust

Enterprise users needed a way to use AI confidently within existing workflows. Initial testing showed users were unsure how AI results were generated and how reliable they were.
